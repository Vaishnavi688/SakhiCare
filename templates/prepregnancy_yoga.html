Yoga & Fitness Tips for Pre-Pregnancy

  • Focus on gentle stretches and avoid overexertion.
  • Practice regularly to improve flexibility and reduce stress.
  • Combine yoga with light cardio and a balanced diet.
  • Listen to your body and rest when needed.
  • Maintain proper breathing and posture throughout exercises.
