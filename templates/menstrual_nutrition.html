🍎 Nutrition for Menstrual Wellness

Eat smart during your period! Proper nutrition helps reduce cramps, boost energy, and improve mood. Here are some recommended foods to support your menstrual health.

Nutrition Tips During Menstruation

  • Drink plenty of water to stay hydrated and reduce bloating.
  • Include iron-rich foods like spinach, lentils, and red meat to prevent fatigue.
  • Eat magnesium-rich foods such as nuts and seeds to ease cramps.
  • Consume complex carbs (oats, brown rice) for sustained energy.
  • Avoid excessive caffeine and sugary snacks to reduce mood swings.
