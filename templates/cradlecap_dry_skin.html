👶 Cradle Cap & Dry Skin

Gentle Care for Your Baby’s Scalp and Skin

Cradle cap and dry skin are common in newborns. While usually harmless, proper care can prevent discomfort and keep your baby’s skin healthy.

💡 Cradle Cap Care:
  • Apply a small amount of baby oil or coconut oil to the scalp before bath.
  • Gently massage the scalp and loosen flakes with a soft brush or comb.
  • Shampoo with a mild, tear-free baby shampoo to remove excess flakes.
🌸 Dry Skin Care:
  • Moisturize your baby’s skin with fragrance-free lotions or creams immediately after bathing.
  • Use lukewarm water instead of hot water for bathing.
  • Avoid harsh soaps or over-bathing, which can worsen dryness.
⚠️ When to Consult a Pediatrician:
  • If redness, swelling, or oozing develops.
  • If flakes are thick, yellow, or persistent despite gentle care.
  • If your baby seems uncomfortable or itchy.
