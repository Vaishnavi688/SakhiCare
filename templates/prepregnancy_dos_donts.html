What To Do & What Not To Do

Your go-to guide for preparing your mind and body for a healthy pregnancy 🌷

✅ What To Do

Eat a Balanced Diet: Include leafy greens, fresh fruits, nuts, and proteins to nourish your body for conception.

Stay Active: Practice light yoga, walking, or stretching to boost fertility and improve mood.

Get Enough Sleep: 7–8 hours of deep rest keeps your hormones balanced and mind refreshed.

Consult Your Doctor: Discuss your health, medications, and supplements with your gynecologist.

Manage Stress: Meditation and mindfulness calm your nerves and prepare you emotionally for motherhood.

🚫 What Not To Do

Avoid Smoking: Nicotine affects egg quality and increases the risk of complications.

Say No to Alcohol: Alcohol disrupts hormones and may affect early embryo development.

Don’t Overstress: Anxiety and overthinking can disturb your hormonal cycle and delay conception.

Cut Down on Junk Food: Greasy, processed foods increase toxins and imbalance your system.

Avoid Late Nights: Irregular sleep patterns affect ovulation and hormone regulation.
