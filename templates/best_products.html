Carefree — Best Products to Use During Periods

💕 Find What Fits You Best, Stay Comfortable & Confident!

Taking care of your menstrual hygiene is all about comfort, safety, and confidence. Every woman’s body is different — so choose what feels right for you. 💕

Here are the most common and trusted menstrual hygiene products you can include in your Carefree kit:

🩸 1️⃣ Sanitary Pads (Napkins)

Best for: Beginners and regular use.

What it is: Soft absorbent pads worn inside underwear to soak up menstrual flow.

Why use it:
• Easy to use and available everywhere.
• Safe for school, work, or travel.
• Comes in various sizes — day pads, night pads, ultra-thin, and extra-long.

Recommended Brands: Whisper, Stayfree, Sofy, Nua, Carmesi, Paree, Niine, Saathi (eco-friendly)

💡 Tip: Change your pad every 4–6 hours to stay fresh and avoid infection.

🌿 2️⃣ Tampons

Best for: Women comfortable with internal products & active lifestyles (swimming, sports).

What it is: Small cylindrical cotton plugs inserted into the vagina to absorb flow internally.

Why use it:
• Feels clean and dry.
• Great for swimming or physical activity.
• Invisible under clothes.

Recommended Brands: OB Pro Comfort, Sirona, Tampax Pearl, Playtex

💡 Tip: Change every 4–8 hours. Wash your hands before and after insertion.

🌸 3️⃣ Menstrual Cups

Best for: Eco-friendly and long-term option.

What it is: A soft, reusable silicone cup inserted inside the vagina to collect menstrual flow.

Why use it:
• Reusable for up to 5–10 years 🌍
• Cost-effective and environmentally friendly.
• Holds more fluid than a pad or tampon.

Recommended Brands: Sirona Cup, Boondh Cup, Carmesi, PeeSafe Cup, Saathi Cup

💡 Tip: Sterilize before and after every period by boiling in hot water.

🌼 4️⃣ Period Panties

Best for: Teens, night-time, and travel comfort.

What it is: Underwear with built-in absorbent layers that soak up menstrual flow directly.

Why use it:
• Super comfortable — feels like normal underwear.
• Leak-proof and reusable.
• Great for light to medium flow.

Recommended Brands: Adira, Carmesi, Modibodi, Zivame, Sirona

💡 Tip: Rinse and wash gently after use. Avoid bleach or harsh detergent.

🌿 5️⃣ Biodegradable / Reusable Cloth Pads

Best for: Eco-conscious women & rural areas where disposable pads are costly.

What it is: Cloth pads made from soft cotton layers that can be washed and reused.

Why use it:
• Safe, breathable, and environment-friendly 🌿
• Cheaper in the long run.
• Made by many women-led rural startups!

Recommended Brands: Eco Femme, Saathi, Vivan, Anandi Pads

💡 Tip: Wash well, dry under sunlight, and store in a clean pouch.

🌷 Carefree Hygiene Tips

✨ Always wash your hands before and after changing.
✨ Keep an extra pad or panty liner in your bag.
✨ Wear cotton underwear to avoid irritation.
✨ Stay hydrated and eat iron-rich foods.
✨ Take a warm bath to relax and reduce cramps.

💬 “Your period is not a problem — it’s a sign of your strength. Care for your body, and it will care for you.”
