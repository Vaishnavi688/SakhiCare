💖 Why Is Vaccinating Your Child Important?

Vaccines Can Save Your Child’s Life

Vaccines help protect your child from serious diseases that can cause long-term health issues or even death. Some diseases, like measles and tetanus, have no cure — vaccination is the safest protection.

Vaccine-Preventable Diseases Are Still Around

Even though vaccines reduced disease rates in India, germs still exist. If fewer people vaccinate, these diseases can return, leading to outbreaks like polio in nearby regions.

Vaccines Protect More Than Just Your Child

When you vaccinate, you help protect your family and community through herd immunity — shielding newborns, patients, and those with weak immune systems.

Vaccines Are Safe and Effective

All vaccines go through strict safety testing and continuous monitoring. Thanks to them, diseases like smallpox and polio are now eliminated in India.

Vaccines Cost Less Than Treating Diseases

Vaccines are far more affordable than hospital treatments. Following your child’s immunization schedule saves you money and safeguards their health long-term.
