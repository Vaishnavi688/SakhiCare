🧷 Diapers & Wipes

Keeping Your Baby Clean, Dry & Comfortable

Choosing the right diapers and wipes is essential for your baby’s comfort and to prevent skin irritation. Breathable diapers and gentle wipes help keep the diaper area dry and healthy.

💡 Choosing Diapers:
  • Opt for soft, breathable, hypoallergenic diapers.
  • Use the right size to prevent leaks and discomfort.
  • Change diapers frequently — at least every 2–3 hours or when soiled.
  • Consider eco-friendly or biodegradable options for sustainability.
💧 Using Wipes Safely:
  • Choose water-based, alcohol-free, fragrance-free wipes.
  • Gently wipe from front to back to prevent infections.
  • Use wipes sparingly — avoid excessive rubbing on sensitive skin.
🌸 Extra Care:
  • Allow the skin to air dry for a few seconds before putting on a new diaper.
  • Apply a thin layer of zinc oxide or diaper cream to prevent rashes.
  • Wash cloth diapers thoroughly with baby-safe detergent.
