👕 Baby Clothing & Blankets

Soft, Comfortable, and Safe Clothing for Your Newborn

Choosing soft, breathable clothing and cozy blankets is crucial for your baby’s comfort and healthy skin. Cotton fabrics are ideal, and proper washing ensures safety for delicate skin.

💡 Choosing Clothes & Blankets:
  • Use 100% cotton clothes and blankets — soft and breathable.
  • Avoid tight-fitting outfits to prevent irritation.
  • Check seams, buttons, and tags to ensure they do not scratch or irritate skin.
  • Wash all new clothes with baby-safe detergent before first use.
🌸 Dressing Tips:
  • Dress your baby in layers that can be added or removed depending on temperature.
  • Use soft socks and mittens to keep hands and feet warm.
  • Choose blankets that are lightweight but warm enough for naps and night sleep.
💧 Extra Care:
  • Avoid fabrics with harsh dyes or chemicals.
  • Always check the baby’s comfort — avoid overheating.
  • Keep blankets away from crib mobiles and loose bedding for safety.
