💖 About SakhiCare

SakhiCare is dedicated to empowering women by providing comprehensive support throughout their reproductive health journey. From tracking menstrual cycles to offering guidance before and after pregnancy, our mission is to ensure every woman has access to the knowledge and resources she needs to live a healthier, happier life.

Our platform also offers curated yoga sessions for pain relief, personalized nutrition advice, and product recommendations for self-care. Additionally, we encourage community engagement through donation opportunities, allowing people to contribute to women’s health initiatives.

At SakhiCare, we believe that informed and supported women create stronger communities. We are committed to making healthcare resources accessible, practical, and user-friendly for women everywhere.

Empowering Rural Women through Digital Health Support

🌸 Our Mission

SakhiCare bridges the healthcare gap for rural women, offering personalized guidance, reminders, and emotional support through technology. We believe every woman deserves easy access to health information and care.

🤝 Our Vision

To create a digitally empowered and health-aware community of women, promoting well-being, confidence, and equality.
