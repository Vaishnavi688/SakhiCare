🧴 Baby Lotion & Oil

Moisturize & Nurture Your Baby’s Delicate Skin

Keeping your baby’s skin soft and hydrated is essential to prevent dryness, rashes, or irritation. Using gentle lotions and oils specifically formulated for newborns helps maintain the natural skin barrier.

💡 Choosing the Right Lotion & Oil:
  • Use natural oils like coconut, almond, or jojoba for massages.
  • Choose fragrance-free or lightly scented lotions.
  • Avoid alcohol-based or harsh chemical products.
  • Brands like Sebamed Baby, Aveeno Baby, and Himalaya Baby are safe options.
  • Always patch-test on a small skin area before regular use.
🌸 Massage & Moisturizing Tips:
  • Gently massage baby’s body for 5–10 minutes after a bath.
  • Focus on elbows, knees, and areas prone to dryness.
  • Apply a thin layer — too much oil can clog pores.
  • Always massage in a warm room to keep your baby comfortable.
💧 Extra Care:
  • Moisturize at least once daily, more during cold weather.
  • Ensure hands are clean before applying lotion or oil.
  • Keep products out of reach of children.
